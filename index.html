CAJU DESIGN

CAJU is a creative development agency specializing in graphic design, art direction, and creative development.

The agency carries a blend of Brazilian references in mind. The name "CAJU" is derived from a Portuguese word for a Brazilian native fruit known as "cashew" in English. The agency aims to utilize this Brazilian heritage background as a dominant approach to design.
